Brand Overview

Ethical armor
for the modern rebel.

Pimpinicchio New York is a sustainable luxury handbag and accessories brand fusing bold aesthetics with ethical craftsmanship. Founded in New York City and rooted in Italian design heritage, every piece is crafted from bio vegan pineapple leather — zero animals, zero compromise.

Guided by the mantra "Boldly Edgy, Unapologetically Sexy," PMPNY designs empower confident, fashion-forward individuals to express themselves without compromise. With neon yellow as its signature code, the brand conveys energy, resilience, and individuality.

Each piece is created in small batches, ensuring quality, longevity, and a commitment to responsible fashion. Stocked at Flying Solo NYC and Wolf & Badger.

The VOLT Concept

A current of
empowerment.

The VOLT Collection ignites the energy of neon yellow — a symbol of power, freedom, and resilience — and channels it into bold, architectural forms. Structured silhouettes are charged with geometric codes: rings, studs, and matelassé patterns that embody strength and protection.

Every detail is crafted from bio vegan pineapple leather, a material that represents both innovation and responsibility. Designed for those who refuse to blend in, VOLT is more than a collection — it's a movement.
