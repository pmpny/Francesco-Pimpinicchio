The Architecture of Aperture

End-to-End Brand Architecture — from sketch to runway.

The Aperture collection represents a shift from traditional accessory design to end-to-end brand architecture. By utilizing a laser-cut structural reveal, the studio highlights the contrast between matte Vegan Pineapple Leather and the high-visibility Volt interior.

Every component — from the custom zinc hardware to the reinforced gussets — was meticulously engineered to ensure precision and high-volume production scalability.
