The Concept

Sustainable Innovation &
Heritage Hardware Engineering.

For this conceptual Versace capsule, the studio explored the intersection of sustainability and luxury. The foundation of the collection utilized regenerated denim, processed into a Japanese Boro patch fabric.

To elevate the upcycled materials, we engineered a complete suite of bespoke hardware — combining traditional hand-drawn ideation with advanced AI rendering to develop high-fidelity digital prototypes of the iconic Barocco 'V' and 'DV' monograms, drastically accelerating the visualization process before physical manufacturing.
