The Role

Bridging design vision and global production.

At Sunday Morning LLC, the primary focus was coordinating international production timelines to ensure prototypes consistently aligned with high-level design direction. This role demanded precision communication across factories, suppliers, and creative teams — building the production management fluency that underpins all PMPNY studio work.

Working within the New York luxury accessories ecosystem for over three years provided deep insight into how international supply chains operate at the premium end of the market — knowledge that directly informs every client project the studio now takes on.
