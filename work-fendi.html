The Concept

"The iconic Fendi logo blends with the most famous apple in the world."

This conceptual capsule explores the collision of Silicon Valley minimalism and Roman opulence. By fusing Fendi's monogram with Apple's industrial design language, the studio developed a 'Punk-Pop' visual identity that bridges high fashion and wearable tech.
