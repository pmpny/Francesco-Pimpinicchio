The Challenge

Translating bold architecture into scalable production.

Cromia is one of Italy's premier luxury handbag manufacturers — known for bold architectural silhouettes that push structural boundaries. The challenge was consistently translating high-concept design into manufacturable products that could scale from sample to global commercial production without losing their structural integrity.

Working directly in the heart of Italy's leather goods district in Tolentino, this role demanded both creative vision and industrial precision — a rare combination that defines the PMPNY studio approach to this day.

The Approach

Engineering the unseen.

The studio engineered complex leather geometries capable of holding their shape flawlessly over time — ensuring high-level conceptual designs could be manufactured efficiently and consistently at global scale. Every collection began with concept development, moved through prototype refinement, and was managed through to supplier production.

Communication with 4+ overseas suppliers required maintaining technical rigor across languages, factories, and supply chains — building the international production fluency that now underpins all PMPNY studio work.
